Interactive Visualizations

These visualizations explore infant mortality as a measure of global development, sex-based health inequality, and long-term health transition. The dashboards allow users to investigate how infant mortality changes across countries, regions, sex, and time while also examining broader global health transitions.

Dashboard 1 — Infant Mortality and Health Transition Explorer

This interactive dashboard combines a global infant mortality map with a health transition scatter plot. Users can filter countries, animate yearly changes, and compare regional development patterns.

Dashboard 2 — Male–Female Infant Mortality Gap

This visualization focuses specifically on the difference between male and female infant mortality rates across countries and over time. Positive values indicate higher male infant mortality, while negative values indicate higher female infant mortality.
